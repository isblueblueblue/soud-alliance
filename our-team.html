Audio Engineering is the Journey

Music & Sound have the power to transport us to another time and place. Sound Alliance loves to harness that power with a broad audience of fellow Show lovers and passionate artists alike. Ever since a early stage, Sound Alliance has found great joy and satisfaction by being involved in the creative show process such as concerts, musicals, plays, films, advertisement, fashion shows and so on.
